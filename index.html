A charity organization dedicated to providing mobility for all

Founded in July of 2023, San Diego Wheelchair Foundation is a nonprofit organization with the mission to enhance the lives of individuals with mobility challenges. Our vision is to create a community where everyone, regardless of physical limitations, can enjoy independence and mobility.

Our Mission
